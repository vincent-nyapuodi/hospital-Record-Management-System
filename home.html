The Healthcare Record System

The goal of the medical healthcare record system is to improve efficiency of the healthcare system

by reducing the overall time and Cost spent creating and retreiving patient records.


The main feature of the Medical Center Health Record System is registering patient medical history

to the database and easily retrieve the data at convenience. The system maintains patient prescriptions

and medical history on the web making the process flow of patient records easy at any time.
